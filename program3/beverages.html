Beverages:

  • Coffee

    • Espresso

      • A strong coffee brewed by forcing steam through finely-ground coffee beans.

    • Cappuccino

      • An espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy, topped with frothy milk.

    • Americano

      • A coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water.

    • Latte

      • A coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

    • Mocha

      • A coffee drink with chocolate flavoring, often topped with whipped cream.

    • Macchiato

      • An espresso with a small amount of milk foam.

    • Flat White

      • An espresso-based coffee drink with velvety microfoam milk.

    • Affogato

      • A dessert coffee made by pouring hot espresso over a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

    • Cold Brew

      • Coffee brewed with cold water over a long period, resulting in a smooth taste.

    • Iced Coffee

      • Chilled coffee served over ice, often sweetened or flavored.

  • Tea

    • Black Tea

      • A type of tea that is more oxidized than green, oolong, and white teas.

    • Green Tea

      • A type of tea that is made from unoxidized leaves and is one of the healthiest beverages.

    • Oolong Tea

      • A traditional Chinese tea known for its floral and fruity aroma.

    • White Tea

      • A delicate tea made from young leaves and buds of the tea plant.

    • Herbal Tea

      • Tea made from herbs, flowers, or spices, often enjoyed for its medicinal properties.

  • Milk

    • A nutrient-rich liquid produced by mammary glands of mammals.
